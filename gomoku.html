Rules

Players alternate turns placing a stone of their color on an empty intersection. Black plays first. The winner is the first player to form an unbroken line of five stones of their color horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. If the board is completely filled and no one can make a line of 5 stones, then the game ends in a draw.
Extracted from: Wikipedia


How to Play

  1. Click on the "Start Game" button on the right to pair with another online player
  2. Once connected, the message dialog will indicate whether it is your turn or not. If yes, then place a stone and wait for opponents response, otherwise, wait paitently for opponent to make a move before taking your turn
  3. Repeat step two until either player quits or forms an unbroken line of five stones
